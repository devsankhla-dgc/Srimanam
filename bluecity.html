Your Local Host

Khamma Ghani Saa! I'm Shiv

"Born and raised in the winding blue lanes of the old city, Jodhpur isn’t just my home—it’s my heritage. I started these walks to bridge the gap between a tourist’s checklist and a local’s life."

My passion is storytelling. Beyond the dates and architecture, I want to share the legends of the Rathore clan, the secrets of the indigo paint, and the best spot for Mirchi Vada that isn’t on Google Maps. When you walk with me, you’re not just a visitor; you’re a guest in my city.
